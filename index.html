Characteristic Load Estimator
for Aerial Acrobatics Rigging Equipment

Methodology:

(Weight of performers + weight of apparatus & rigging equipment) × dynamic load factor × safety factor of 6 (from ANSI 1.43 2016 Entertainment Technology — Performer Flying Systems Subsection 4.6.5).

Example:

(440lbs performers + 10lbs trapeze) × 3 dynamic load max factor × 6 ANSI safety factor = 8100lbs. max safety trapeze performers

Disclaimer:

This is just an estimate of possible dynamic loads in a rigging system that travels straight down from an anchor point. This does not take into account orbital motion which puts different strains on rigging equipment. For a more accurate estimate please use a load cell sensor while running your act to determine your actual dynamic loads.
